What is a mechanical keyboard?

A mechanical keyboard is comprised of 5 major components:

  1. The case
  2. Programmable pcb
  3. Mounting plate
  4. Keyswitch
  5. Key cap

1. Customisable case: Give your mechanical keyboard a personalised look and feel

2. Programmable PCB: Dictate the functions your keyboard performs based on your usage and preferences

3. Mounting plate: Choose from a range of typing sounds to be produced while you work

4. Keyswitch: Customise how the keyboard feels when you type - linear, tactile, or clicky

5. Key cap: Show off your personal style and colour choices!
